# Ihsan Agile Templates

Practical implementation tools for adopting Ihsan Agile practices.

## Available Templates

### Core Practices

- [Niyyah Check-in Template](./niyyah-checkin-template.md) – Intention-setting at sprint planning (2–3 min)
- [Ethical Definition of Done](./ethical-dod-checklist.md) – Comprehensive DoD with Islamic values
- [Muhāsabah Retrospective Template](./muhasabah-retrospective-template.md) – Ethical reflection and growth (45–90 min)

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Start simple:** Begin with Niyyah check-ins
2. **Adapt freely:** Modify for your context
3. **Share learnings:** Contribute improvements via PR

## Template Philosophy

These templates operationalize the Three Pillars:
- **Niyyah** (Intention) - Why are we building this?
- **Iḥsān** (Excellence) - Are we doing this with God-consciousness?
- **Maṣlaḥah** (Public Good) - Who benefits and how?
